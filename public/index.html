Heart rate is the number of times your heart beats in a minute. An average heart rate is 60 to 100 beats per minute.

Blood oxygen levels, otherwise known as SpO2 levels, is the percentage of oxygen saturated in your blood. The average SpO2 levels is 95% or higher.

Diastolic blood pressure measures the pressure in your arteries when your heart rests between beats. Systolic blood pressure measures the pressure in your arteries when your heart beats. Your blood pressure is the systolic over diastolic and is measured in millimeters of mercury.
